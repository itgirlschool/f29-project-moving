SERVICES

Estimate of work size

Free visit to the client's address to provide an estimate of the size of the work, indicate the client's wishes in the contract, estimate the cost of services.

Labor only

If you don't need a truck, but only the services of movers. Moving items indoors, loading and unloading the rented truck, moving inside the building.

Loading and, or unloading service

When you only need help loading your items into a rental truck, portable storage unit (Pod, shipping container, etc.), cargo trailer or truck U-Haul, etc. When all you need is help. Unloading your belongings into your new home or storage unit from your rental truck, portable storage unit (Pod, shipping container, etc.), cargo trailer or truck U-Haul, etc.

Shuttle in the same facility

When you need to move to a new apartment in the same complex using special equipment.

Assemble and disassemble furniture

Furniture disassembly and assembly services are free for each client. We have professional tools and extensive experience.

Local Moving

When you need to move within a 100-mile radius, we can provide a 16- to 26-foot moving truck, depending on your needs.

Long Distance Mooving

When you need to move within a radius of more than 100 miles. This service requires more professionalism and experience. And our team is ready to help you.
